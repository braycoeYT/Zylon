Secret Seed System:
While waiting for 1.4.5, I've added a workaround to allow for mixing and matching secret seeds. You can add any of the following codes to your world's NAME (not seed) and it will force that secret seed to occur.
Abyssworld - ":b:"
Autumn - ":a:"
Turf War - ":t:"

A content mod I've been developing on and off since January 2020. Some cool stuff the mod adds includes summon critical strike support and blowpipes being reworked into their own unique subclass! Warning: Mod does not work in multiplayer.

Secret Seed System:
While waiting for 1.4.5, I've added a workaround to allow for mixing and matching secret seeds. You can add any of the following codes to your world's NAME (not seed) and it will force that secret seed to occur.
Abyssworld - "/b"
Autumn - "/a"
Turf War - "/t"
